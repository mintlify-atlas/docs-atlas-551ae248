## What is NEAR?

NEAR is a **user-friendly** and **carbon-neutral** blockchain, built to be fast, secure, and infinitely scalable. It offers a simple user experience with named accounts, low fees, and a robust developer ecosystem.

In technical terms, NEAR is a **layer-1**, **sharded**, **proof-of-stake** blockchain built with usability in mind. In simpler terms, NEAR is the **blockchain for everyone**.

<Note>
  NEAR has achieved 5 years of **100% uptime** and processed over **4 billion transactions** with peaks exceeding 13M transactions in a single day.
</Note>

## Why Choose NEAR?

NEAR is a technical marvel offering unique features that make building and using blockchain applications easier than ever:

### User Experience First

- **Named Accounts**: Use human-readable accounts like `alice.near` instead of cryptographic hashes
- **Fast & Cheap**: Transactions finalize in ~1.3 seconds and cost less than 1¢ in fees
- **Easy Onboarding**: Create accounts for free, login with social accounts or Telegram
- **Account Abstraction**: Users don't need to buy crypto to get started
- **Access Keys**: Secure and flexible permission system for managing accounts
- **Chain Signatures**: Control accounts on other blockchains directly from NEAR

### Production Ready

NEAR has proven its reliability at scale:

- **100% uptime** over 5 years of operation
- **4+ billion transactions** processed successfully
- **13M+ transactions** handled in peak single-day load
- Home to apps with **millions of active users** including Kai-ching, Sweat, and Hot Wallet

### Developer Experience

- Build contracts in **JavaScript** or **Rust**
- Comprehensive documentation with practical examples
- Active developer community and office hours
- **EVM compatible** via Project Aurora for easy Solidity contract deployment
- Earn revenue from your contract's gas fees

## Technical Architecture

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Layer-1 Blockchain">
    NEAR is the foundation that supports everything else built on it. It keeps all transaction records safe and unchangeable, which keeps the network secure and trustworthy.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Sharded Design">
    The network is broken into pieces (shards) that work in parallel. This helps NEAR process transactions quickly and efficiently, enabling infinite scalability.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Proof-of-Stake Consensus">
    NEAR uses proof-of-stake, which consumes far less electricity compared to proof-of-work blockchains. Validators stake NEAR tokens to help secure the network, making it more accessible and environmentally friendly.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
